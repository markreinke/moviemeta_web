Welcome to Project Moviemeta!
We're interested in "What's in the minds across the whole world? What can we learn from the movie topics?".
Here, you can find interesting insights on the movie topics across the world.
Popular movies for a specific country should contain social, political meaning on that country/society.
We investigated topic distributions of popular movies for United States, Germany, United Kingdom, and South Korea.
We selected those four countries to see if there are any similarity or uniqueness among those countries.
