What are the popular movie topics across countries?

Topics of popular movies is an important indicator of interests or issues of a society, because making and selecting movie are inevitably affected by social, political, and historical background. To answer our first question, 2,400 popular movie plots for United States, United Kingdom, Germany, Russia, Mexico, and South Korea were analyzed since 2007(50 popular movies per year, per country). Global popular topics represent the 'overall' popularity of topics across the 6 countries. As we can see in the following chart, topic of 'love, relationships' is the most popular, along with 'love, family', 'exploration, nature, space', 'friendship, relationships', and 'society, culture'. Interestingly, 3 out of top 5 topics represent 'relationship' with others(love, family, friendship), indicating that the 'relationship' is in the heart of our life. Prevalent words for each topics, such as love, woman, friend, marriage, wife, father, and mother, also indicate that relationship with family, friend, and spouse is a utmost important issues. One interesting point was observed by comparing the 6 countries : unlike other 5 countries, the most popular topic in South Korea is 'exploration, nature, space'. It can attributed to the South Korean moviegoers' preference toward hollywood blockbusters.
